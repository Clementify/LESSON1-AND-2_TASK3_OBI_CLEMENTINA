Liquid Soap Production

These ingredients are for large-scale production of liquid soaps like Morning Fresh; if you intend to make liquid soap at home, kindly refer to procedure 1. 700 kg of water, 70 kg of sulfuric acid, Sodium carbonate (washing soda), 1 kg of triethanolamine, 55 kg of texapon, 55 grams of glycerine, 1 kg of trisodium phosphate, 4 grams of soap colouring, 1 kg soap preservative, Perfume Salt

Step1

Install a funnel at the bottom of a big, high-temperature-resistant container or pot to filter and drain the precipitated liquid soap contaminants. Install a second faucet 17 cm from the pot's base for subsequent use in filling the liquid soap. Put 700 kg of water in the pot. Add sodium phosphate to the water and mix the ingredients in a single direction. Add the sulfonic acid gradually while continuing to stir in the same direction as previously to prevent the formation of lather that would ruin the liquid soap and alter the outcomes of the interactions between the constituent ingredients.

Step2

After ensuring the sulfonic acid is dissolved, continue stirring in one direction while adding sodium carbonate. Add triethanolamine until the soap ingredients are neutralised, and the reaction is complete. Leave the mixture alone until its temperature, which must have increased due to chemical interaction, has cooled. Now place the mixture in a big plastic container. Then, include the 55 kg of texapon, 10 kg of salt, and 70 litres of water with a long wooden stick.

Step3

Now whisk in 1 kg of soap preservative in the same direction. After ensuring that all the ingredients are homogenous, add glycerine, colourant, and fragrance until all the liquid soap-making procedures have been finished. Store your morning fresh liquid soap in plastic bottles.

Powdered Detergent Production

Each company has its detergent powder making formula. Depending on your target market demographic, you have to craft a unique formula. You can source the needed manufacturing technology from several researchers and schools. To help you, we’ve put together this reference for your basic formula and ingredients percentage.

Ingredients 85% active LAB acid slurry Sodium carbonate (soda ash) Sodium metasilicate Alkaline sodium silicate Sodium bicarbonate Sodium Sulphate (anhydrous) Sodium tripolyphosphate Sodium carboxymethyl cellulose Phthalocyanine blue color or oil-soluble yellow color Optical whitener Perfume Water

Step 1

Using soda ash, neutralize the acid slurry. Then, so that the reaction is completed, keep the mixture for about an hour.

Step2

Mix all the other ingredients like Colours, Glauber’s salt, CMC, STEP, Perfume, TSP, and many others. Once done, continuously mix everything so that your ingredients blend to the neutralized acid slurry.

Step 3

Afterward, dry the mixture. Lastly, pack your finished product. Make sure to use proper packing.
